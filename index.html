Getting Here

Easy to reach from
anywhere in Bangalore

Strategically located at Budigere Cross on the Old Madras Road corridor, FerroHub Mall is well-connected by road and public transport.

🚗

By Car / Cab

Easily locatable on Google Maps. Located on Old Madras Road near Budigere Cross junction. 600+ free parking spots with valet service.

KR Puram — 12 min Whitefield — 20 min Indiranagar — 30 min
🚌

By Bus

BMTC buses along Old Madras Road stop at Budigere Cross. Multiple routes connect from Majestic, KR Puram, and Whitefield.

Nearest stop: Budigere Cross Routes: 331, 333, 500 series
🚇

By Metro

The Purple Line extension towards Whitefield brings metro connectivity closer. Take an auto from the nearest metro station.

Nearest: KR Puram Metro Auto from station — 15 min
✈️

From Airport

Kempegowda International Airport is connected via the Outer Ring Road and Old Madras Road. Airport taxis and rideshare available.

KIA Airport — ~45 min Via ORR / Old Madras Road

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

What are the mall hours?▾
We're open 10 AM to 10 PM every day. During festivals and special events, we extend our hours. Check our events section for holiday schedules.
Is parking free?▾
Yes, completely free for all visitors. We have 600+ parking spots across the mall, and complimentary valet service is available during peak hours.
Can I bring my pet?▾
Absolutely! Pets are welcome throughout the outdoor areas. Service animals are permitted everywhere. We just ask that you keep pets leashed for everyone's comfort.
Is the mall wheelchair accessible?▾
Fully accessible. Ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms are available throughout. Free wheelchair rentals are available at the information desk.
What if it rains?▾
Covered walkways connect all major areas. Complimentary umbrellas are available at the info desk. Most dining and retail spaces have full shelter.
Is entry free?▾
Yes! Entry to FerroHub Mall is completely free for everyone. Just walk in and enjoy.
